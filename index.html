Welcome

This website was created with the intention of uniting the two seemingly distinct fields of sport and physics, in a way which is accessible to viewers with or without prior physics knowledge. The information presented to you will help demonstrate how techniques, equipment, and strategies in commonly played sports, have been carefully formulated using physical concepts such as the Magnus effect and Bernoulli principle. At the end of each section there is an interactive opportunity to test your understanding about this important role physics has in enhancing your favourite sports. All that is left to say is, delve in, learn, and enjoy!

Aerodynamics in Sport - Site Guide

Welcome!

The world of sport attracts the attention of billions across the world. Many may not realise how the study of physics impacts their favourite sports. In this site, we want to show the innate links between the study of aerodynamics and ball sports, including football, tennis, cricket, and golf.

Site Features

Thoughout the site bold text indicates key words which you may wish to know more about.

Clicking on these bits of bold text will open small pop-ups like this one to give you quick insights into the topics being discussed.

They may also contain links to pages with more detailed information on the topic, so if you wish to know more about it you can easily switch to that page.
